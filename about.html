About Nandana

Hello, I'm Nandana, and you don't have to figure it all out alone.

I'm a certified NLP coach and life coach specialising in stress management for women. I understand how challenging life transitions can be, whether moving to a new country, becoming a mother, or simply carrying the weight of daily stress.

My passion is supporting women in finding calm, confidence and emotional strength. Drawing on Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) and practical coaching tools, I create a safe, supportive space where you can talk openly and feel empowered to move forward.

You are not alone, and support is available.
